Seaweed Muffins

Description

Don't let the taste of the ocean scare you, these muffins will bring a sailor's smile during dessert time.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 6 tablespoons salted butter, softened
  • ¼ cup chopped seaweed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sea salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons honey (Optional)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray muffin cups with cooking spray.
  2. Mix butter, seaweed, and sea salt together in a bowl until smooth. Mix flour, confectioners' sugar, eggs, honey, coconut oil, and cinnamon into butter mixture until batter is just mixed; pour into the prepared muffin cups.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the sides of the muffins begin to darken, 12 to 15 minutes.
